How To Play

  • Roll a 6 to move a token out of the base.
  • Roll a 6 grants an extra turn. Rolling three 6s in a row passes the turn.
  • Landing on an opponent's token sends it back to base and grants you an extra turn.
  • Spaces marked with ★ are safe zones.
  • Getting a token Home grants an auto extra turn.
  • First player to get all 4 tokens Home wins!
